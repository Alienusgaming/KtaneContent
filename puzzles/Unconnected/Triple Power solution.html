The phrase “get up from the table” in the flavour text suggests to move up one entry in the table (list) in the Ingredients manual, giving the arrangement shown on the right.

The puzzle’s arrangement of items resembling the keys on a keypad, and the phrase “locked up” in the flavour text, together hint at the module Keypad Lock. Furthermore, in addition to the overtly presented serial number, the puzzle’s title suggests to assume three batteries are present. The solution to the Keypad Lock module would then be:
